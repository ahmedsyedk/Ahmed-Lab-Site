Where Molecules Become Medicines

Designing the next generation of small-molecule therapeutics, one bond at a time.

Our research interests include bioorganic and medicinal chemistry, structure/ligand-based drug design and development, biorthogonal chemistry, heterocyclic chemistry, mechanistic study of novel small-molecule modulators of diverse range of therapeutic targets, and development of new synthetic methodologies.
